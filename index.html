ABOUT ME

     Hongyue Wu is an Assistant Professor at the College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University. After having been a joint Ph.D. student at the University of Sydney from 2017 to 2018, he received his Ph.D. from the college of computer science and technology, Zhejiang University, and joined the College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University in 2018.

 

         His research interests include service computing, mobile edge computing, and cloud computing. He has published more than 10 papers, including international journal papers published on IEEE TPDS, TSC, TASE, ect., and top international conference papers published on ICSOC, IEEE ICWS, etc. He has one paper published on ICSOC 17 and won the Sole Best Paper Award, which is the first time for Chinese to win this award. He has published one English book. He has won many awards during his Ph.D. period, including National Scholarship for Doctoral Candidate, Excellent Postgraduate Student’s Award, etc.
